Road accident deaths remain at record lows

Final stats for 2022 showed 140 people were killed in reported road accidents, one fewer than the previous year. The report showed a long-term downward trend with the number of people killed on roads decreasing by 57% since 2000. However, overall casualties did rise slightly in 2021 to 5,103, from a record low in 2020 of 5,056. The highest rate was for men aged between 17 and 25, with a rate of 1.9 accident per 1,000 of the population, one and half times more than the rate for women of the same age.
